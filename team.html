About RADHA FOUNDATION

Radha Foundation is a proprietorship concern of Mrs. Radha Nathyal Memorial Foundation. Foundation is a section 25 company under Companies act 2013 for social/charitable purposes. It has been established in memory of Mrs Radha Rani Nathyal who expired on 5th of May, 2021 during second Covid wave.
