OUR FEATURES

Join us To Know Our Projects

Career Awareness

Career Counselling Sessions

We conduct sessions on Conventional and Non-Conventional Careers across schools in India. These sessions enable young students groom themselves into successful professionals.

Stream Selection & Support

Helping Students choose the Right Stream.

Every year, we help thousands of students to choose the right stream for themselves and we further help them with Complete Career Trajectory.

Educational Conclaves

Edu-Leaders Summits

We successfully conduct Principals' Conclaves, Counsellors Meets and various Edu-Leaders Summits accross the country. We organise several Training Sessions for teachers at Schools and Colleges.

Social Awareness Campaigns

Careers and Education related programs

It is our constant endeavour to educate Students, Parents, Educators & other Stake-Holders and also make them aware about latest career options, new-age skills and latest opportunities.

About RADHA FOUNDATION

Radha Foundation is a proprietorship concern of Mrs. Radha Nathyal Memorial Foundation. Foundation is a section 25 company under Companies act 2013 for social/charitable purposes. It has been established in memory of Mrs Radha Rani Nathyal who expired on 5th of May, 2021 during second Covid wave.
